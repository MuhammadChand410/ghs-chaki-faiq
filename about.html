About Our School

Government High School Chaki Faiq has been a beacon of educational excellence for over two decades, shaping young minds and building future leaders of our community.

Our Story

Established in 1999, Government High School Chaki Faiq began with a vision to provide quality education to the children of our local community. What started as a small institution with just 150 students has grown into a thriving educational center serving over 1,200 students.

Our school is committed to academic excellence while nurturing the overall development of each student. We believe in creating an environment where students can explore their potential, develop critical thinking skills, and prepare for the challenges of tomorrow.

Located in the heart of Chaki Faiq, our school serves as a cornerstone of the community, fostering not just academic growth but also social responsibility and civic engagement.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive education that develops intellectual capabilities, moral character, and practical skills essential for success in higher education and life.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that nurtures confident, responsible, and innovative global citizens who contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Values

  • Excellence in Education
  • Integrity and Honesty
  • Respect and Tolerance
  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Community Service
