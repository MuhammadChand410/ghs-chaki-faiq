Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Students need to submit their previous academic records, birth certificate, and other required documents. Specific requirements vary by class level.

How can I get my child's transfer certificate?

Submit a written application to the principal's office with required documents. Processing typically takes 3-5 working days.

Are transportation facilities available?

We provide school transport services to various routes. Contact our transport coordinator for route details and availability.

What are the school timings?

Morning session (Classes 6-8): 8:00 AM - 12:15 PM

Afternoon session (Classes 9-10): 1:00 PM - 5:15 PM

How can parents track student progress?

Regular parent-teacher meetings are conducted, and progress reports are shared. Parents can also schedule individual meetings with teachers.

What extracurricular activities are offered?

We offer various clubs including Science Club, Debate Society, Sports teams, Arts & Crafts, and community service programs.
